Pasta alla Vodka

Description

This pasta alla vodka recipe is easy to throw together for a quick dinner any night of the week â€” the sauce can be made while you cook the pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1 pint heavy cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, breaking up with a fork. Stir in basil, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes. Stir in vodka and cook 15 minutes more.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Stir cream into sauce; cook 10 minutes more. Toss with hot pasta.

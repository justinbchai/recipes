Chinese Stir-Fried Egg and Tomato

Description

This is one of the most traditional Chinese recipes that happens to be vegetarian. You will never go wrong with egg and tomato combination.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into 1-inch wedges
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add eggs and green onions. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, about 10 seconds. Immediately scrape eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add tomatoes to the skillet. Saute, stirring occasionally, until juices are released and tomatoes are slightly wilted, about 5 minutes. Add salt and sugar; stir to mix well. Return eggs to skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set, 2 to 3 minutes.
